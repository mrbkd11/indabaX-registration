IndabaX Tunisia 2023

Join us for 2 days packed with panels, workshops, hackathons, talks and more in the 4th edition of IndabaX Tunisia! If you’re an AI enthusiast, you cannot miss this event!

May 13th and 14th, 2023

Sup’com, Technopole El Ghazela

About IndabaX 2023 IndabaX Tunisia is a local Deep Learning Indaba that brings together professionals and AI enthusiasts to exchange ideas, network, and learn about the latest developments in the Artificial Intelligence field. At this two-day event, attendees can expect to engage in keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and a 20-hour hackathon. For its 4th edition, IEEE Sup'Com Student Branch and InstaDeep are collaborating to deliver an event that meets your expectations and offers a variety of opportunities to expand your network and deepen your understanding of the AI field.

In partnership with ZINDI

Zindi is the first data science competition platform in Africa. Zindi hosts an entire data science ecosystem of scientists, engineers, academics, companies, NGOs, governments and institutions focused on solving Africa’s most pressing problems.

20-hour hackathon for you to test your abilities

The 20-hour hackathon will focus on Biotechnologies and will be hosted on Zindi’s platform. It is a unique chance for data scientists and engineers to demonstrate their expertise to solve real world problems related to the intersection of biology and technology. Participants in the hackathon will be challenged to analyze datasets with a limited amount of time to complete the task.

Oliver Bent

Senior Software Engineer at InstaDeep

×

Biography:

Oliver Bent is an accomplished researcher with a DPhil from the AIMS CDT (Autonomous Intelligent Machines and Systems) program at the University of Oxford, and currently serves as a senior software engineer on DeepChain core technologies at InstaDeep. His research has focused on the application of machine learning for decision making from computationally expensive simulation models. Oliver also holds his Master of Engineering Science (M.Eng.) degree from the University of Oxford and has worked with numerous organisations internationally, gaining practical experience in applying machine learning to high-impact real-world scenarios. He is dedicated to leveraging machine learning to drive advancements in various domains, including diseases, healthcare and education.

Arnu Pretorius

Senior Research Scientist at InstaDeep

×

Biography:

Arnu Pretorius is a senior research scientist specializing in Reinforcement Learning at InstaDeep, with a PhD in Computer Science from Stellenbosch University. His doctoral thesis extensively explored the complexities of noise regularized neural networks, with a keen focus on topics such as initialization, learning, and inference. Based in Cape Town, South Africa, Arnu is actively involved in cutting-edge research and development efforts related to multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL), collaborating closely with applied teams to tackle real-world challenges at scale. He is passionate about AI skills development in Africa and is regularly involved in initiatives that aim to strengthen AI talent across the continent.

Mohamed Ould-Elhassen Aoueileyine

Assistant professor and cofounder of Tech4IoT

×

Biography:

Dr. Mohamed Ould-Elhassen Aoueileyine is an Assistant Professor at the University of Carthage with expertise in ICT, Trainer and Consultant specifically in the fields of IoT, TinyML, eHealth, AI, and Sensor Network. With a strong background in electrical engineering, Mohamed has a deep understanding of the technical aspects of these technologies and how they can be applied to real-world problems. Mohamed's research has focused on a variety of topics within these fields, including the development of smart systems for eHealth, the integration of TinyML in IoT devices, and the security of IoT networks. Mohamed is also working on professional projects related to electrical mobility, as well as embedded Linux for nanosatellites. As an accomplished researcher and educator, Mohamed has published numerous papers and articles in leading academic journals and conferences. He is also an active member of professional organizations in his field such as UIT, AUF and AICTO, and regularly participate in events and conferences to share their expertise and collaborate with other experts in the field.
